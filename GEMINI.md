# 🤖 AUTOFLOCK - AI Automation & Social Publishing

## Project Purpose
Automate the publishing of updates about AI tools, agents, terminal tricks, images, videos, prompts, and builds.

## Distribution Channels
- **Telegram**: Channel/Bot delivery.
- **WhatsApp**: Community/Group updates.
- **Facebook**: Page/Group postings.

## Core Mandates
1. **Focus**: Limited to AI/Terminal/Automation content. No general news.
2. **Format**: Optimized for social media (short, impactful, visual).
3. **Frequency**: Configurable via `auto_flock.py`.
4. **Style**: Highly technical but accessible terminal tricks and agent workflows.

## Pipeline (Sheep)
1. **Sheep 1 (Researcher)**: Finds new AI tools, terminal tricks, and prompts.
2. **Sheep 2 (Curator)**: Filters for high-value content.
3. **Sheep 3 (Prompt Engineer)**: Generates prompts for images/videos.
4. **Sheep 4 (Media Generator)**: Creates/Downloads visual assets (placeholders or API).
5. **Sheep 5 (Content Writer)**: Writes social-friendly captions (Emoji-rich).
6. **Sheep 6 (Publisher - Telegram)**: Sends to Telegram API.
7. **Sheep 7 (Publisher - WhatsApp)**: Sends to WhatsApp (via Proxy/Bot).
8. **Sheep 8 (Publisher - Facebook)**: Posts to FB Page/Group.
9. **Sheep 9 (Auditor)**: Logs performance and success rates.
