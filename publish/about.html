About Us

Auto Flock is a news publication curated by Flock+, a dedicated team of journalists, technologists, and content enthusiasts committed to delivering timely updates.

We leverage advanced AI summarization technology to scan global news sources, but we believe in the "human-in-the-loop" principle. Every piece of content published on our platform is reviewed, fact-checked, and finalized by our human editors to ensure accuracy, context, and editorial integrity.

Our mission is to provide you with the most essential stories of the day, distilled for clarity and speed, without sacrificing the standards of professional journalism.
