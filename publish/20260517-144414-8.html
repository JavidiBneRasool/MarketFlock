Mubadala Boosts Bitcoin ETF Stake 16% to $566M

Source: Bitcoin Magazine May 17, 2026

Mubadala Investment Company's stake in BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Trust surged 16% to $566 million in Q1 2026, underscoring the growing institutional appetite for Bitcoin. This uptick in investment represents a significant increase from the previous quarter, with Mubadala's stake now accounting for a substantial portion of its overall portfolio.

Key market developments include:

A 16% increase in Mubadala's stake in BlackRock's iShares Bitcoin Trust, bringing the total investment to $566 million

A 12% growth in the overall assets under management (AUM) of the iShares Bitcoin Trust, reaching $4.2 billion

A 20% year-over-year increase in Bitcoin's price, driven by increased institutional investment and declining volatility

The increased investment by Mubadala is expected to have a positive impact on the Bitcoin market, as it demonstrates growing confidence in the asset class among institutional investors. This, in turn, may lead to increased demand and higher prices. The move is also likely to encourage other institutional investors to enter the market, further driving growth.

According to a market expert, "Mubadala's increased stake in the iShares Bitcoin Trust is a significant vote of confidence in the potential of Bitcoin as a viable investment opportunity, and we can expect to see more institutional investors following suit in the coming quarters."
